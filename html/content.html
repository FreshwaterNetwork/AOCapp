Fish and wildlife populations and habitats in the Lower Green Bay and Fox River Area of Concern have been impaired and need help. Recovery needs to include habitat restoration in and near the AOC, watershed wetland restoration to improve water quality, and reconnecting stream networks for fish and aquatic life. See less...
